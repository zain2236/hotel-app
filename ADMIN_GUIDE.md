## ‚ûï How to Add Rooms

1. **Login as Admin** (see above)
2. In the sidebar, click **"Add Room"** or go to **"Rooms"** ‚Üí **"Add New Room"**
3. Fill in the room form:
   - **Room Title** (e.g., "Deluxe Ocean View Suite")
   - **Description** (detailed room description)
   - **Price per Night** (e.g., 299.99)
   - **Capacity** (number of guests, e.g., 2)
   - **Room Type** (Standard, Deluxe, Suite, Presidential)
   - **Upload Image** (optional - recommended size: 800x600px)
   - **Amenities** (comma separated, e.g., "WiFi, TV, AC, Mini Bar")
   - **Available** checkbox (check if room is ready for booking)
4. Click **"Create Room"**
5. Room will appear on the website immediately!

## ‚úÖ How to Approve Booking Requests

### Method 1: Quick Approve from Bookings List
1. Go to **"Bookings"** in the sidebar
2. Find the pending booking (yellow badge)
3. Click the green **‚úì Approve** button
4. Confirm the action
5. Booking status changes to "Confirmed" (green badge)

### Method 2: Approve from Booking Details
1. Go to **"Bookings"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"View"** (eye icon) on any booking
3. Click **"Approve Booking"** button (green button)
4. Confirm the action

### Method 3: Edit and Approve
1. Go to **"Bookings"** ‚Üí Click **"Edit"** (pencil icon)
2. Change status to **"Confirmed"**
3. Click **"Update Booking"**

## üìä Booking Statuses Explained

- **Pending** (Yellow) - New booking, waiting for admin approval
- **Confirmed** (Green) - Approved by admin, booking is confirmed
- **Cancelled** (Red) - Booking was cancelled
- **Completed** (Blue) - Guest stay has been completed
